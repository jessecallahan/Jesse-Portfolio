Beep Boop

Enter a number into the beep boop machine:
  • Numbers with a 1 in them "beep"
  • Numbers with a 2 "boop"
  • Numbers with a 3 "sorry dude"

This and Ping Pong generate a list of numbers based on parameters. Try it out! Check out the specs and ReadMe: https://github.com/jessecallahan/Jesse-Portfolio/blob/master/readme/READMEbeep.md
