Pandemic Game

This is one of the first games I created. I used setInterval() and setTimeout(). Also this was made using npm webpacks and I used Jest to test different parts of behaviors before I started the UI.

    Rules:
  • 0% you win, 100% you lose
  • Three lines of defense: One at 10 days, Two at 30 days, and 3 at 150 days
