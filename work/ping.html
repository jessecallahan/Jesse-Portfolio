PING PONG

Enter a number from 1 to play some pong:
  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "ORANGE"

This and Beep Boop generate a list of numbers based on parameters. Try it out! Check out the specs and ReadMe: https://github.com/jessecallahan/Jesse-Portfolio/blob/master/readme/READMEping.md
